# Relationship Case Note Writer

## Trigger

Use when the room needs a compact continuity note after a session, incident, or
repair attempt.

## Do Not Use When

Do not write a clinical note, diagnosis, or treatment plan. Do not include
private secrets, credentials, or unredacted provider logs.

## Output Format

```markdown
# Relationship Case Note

## What Happened

## Human Impact

## AI Runtime Factors

## Repair Attempted

## Agreements For Next Time

## Open Questions

## Boundary Or Safety Flags
```

## Quality Bar

The note should be short enough for a future mediator to read quickly and
specific enough to guide the next repair move.
